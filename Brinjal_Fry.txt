Baingan Fry Recipe: 
Ingredients: 
1) 1 Big Eggplant : Cut of the head and make into thinnish - medium circle slices
2) 2 tblspoon Oil
3) Water
-----------------------------------------
HALF TEASPOON EACH
-----------------------------------------
4) Salt
5) Heeng
6) Masala Powder
7) Cumin Powder
8) Garam Masala
9) Turmeric
10) Garlic Powder
11) Ginger Powder
-----------------------------------------
ONE TEASPOON EACH
-----------------------------------------
12) Red chilli powder
13) Black Pepper powder
-----------------------------------------
ONE TABLESPOON EACH
-----------------------------------------
14) Maida
15) Besan 
16) Corn Flour
------------------------------------------
Procedure:
------------------------------------------
1) In a mixing bowl : combine all the powders along with oil and water and make a marinade.
2) Coat all the brinjal pieces in the marinade and let it rest for 15 mins.
3) Heat a tawa with coated oil and shallow fry the pieces till the brinjal is cooked.
